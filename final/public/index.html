Predict the latest story by NY Times







  • Choose one of the 4 possible categories of the next NY Times Article

  • The Categories are on the box

  • Press "Start New Game" when ready
  • Wait for the Blue light to turn on

  • Once the light is Blue, press one of the 4 buttons on the box to choose answer



  • If you answer correctly, the green light will switch on.

  • If you didn't, the red light will switch on.
